IITR Lingo

Chapo

short form of Chai-pakoda. Used to Refer to a party.

Despo

short for desperate. One could be a despo for money, food, or bandi

Ghissu

A person who does ghissai all the time. eg. Ghissu log exam ke time bahut kaam aate hain!

GPL

G**d pe laat. :(

kholu

opener. Eg. Electrical ka kholu kaun h?.

Phodu

a person who has done something really well. Bond can also be spelled as fodu. .

Matka

MTech student (also, matki) or for any general PG.

Junta

More than one person.

CG

stands for CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average).

Backchodi

gossips of no use.

Room-Baap

Room baap is the senior who stayed in the same room in which you are staying in 1st year.
